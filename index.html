Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving creativity and technological advancements to foster growth and development in Bangladesh.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices for long-term environmental and economic stability.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient infrastructure to support urban and rural development.

Empowerment

Enhancing opportunities for individuals through education, skills, and economic independence.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Green Energy

Green energy looks set to be part of the future of the world, offering a cleaner alternative to many of today’s energy sources. Readily replenished, these energy sources are not just good for the environment, but are also leading to job creation and look set to become economically viable as developments continue.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure technology represents a paradigm shift in how enterprises design, deploy, and manage their IT environments to meet the dynamic demands of the digital era. At its core, it embodies the principles of flexibility, scalability, and efficiency.
